IMPOSTER

Who's The Spy?

How To Play

1

Add Players

Add at least 3 players. Each player can add a photo or take one with the camera.

2

Secret Word

Everyone gets the same secret word — except the Imposter(s), who only get a vague hint!

3

Reveal Phase

Pass the phone around. Each player swipes up to see their word (or find out they're the imposter). Then pass it to the next player.

4

Discussion

A timer starts. Players ask each other questions to figure out who the Imposter is. The Imposter tries to blend in!

5

Vote!

When time runs out, verbally vote on who the Imposter is. Then tap "Reveal Imposter" to see who it was!

6

Imposter Wins If...

They aren't caught! Or if they can correctly guess the secret word after being revealed.
